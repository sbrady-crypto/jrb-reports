Framework

What are BDI & CDI?

Category Development Index (CDI)

Measures how strong beauty/cosmetics category demand is in a state relative to the national average. A CDI of 1.10 means that state has 10% more category demand than average.

Brand Development Index (BDI)

Measures how strong JRB brand interest is in a state relative to the national average. A BDI of 0.71 means JRB awareness is 29% below average in that state.

Quadrant Overview

Four types of markets

Low CDI, High BDI

Brand surplus. JRB is well-known but category demand is soft. 27 states live here — mostly Northeast & Mountain West. Protect, don't over-invest.

High CDI, High BDI

Brand + category strength. IL, NJ, NY. JRB is strong where the category is strong. Maximize share & defend.

Low CDI, Low BDI

Double gap. 11 states with weak category demand AND weak JRB awareness. Deprioritize — hard to move both levers at once.

High CDI, Low BDI

Category surplus = opportunity. People are buying beauty — just not JRB. This is where we focus.

Key Learnings

What the data tells us

  • CA + TX = 25% of category demand but only 18.5% of JRB revenue. These two states alone account for ~$1.9M in unrealized Q1 revenue. The category is buying beauty — they're just not buying JRB.
  • Florida is a sleeping giant. Nearly 7% of category demand, BDI of 0.92. JRB under-indexes by $374K. A large retiree/mature beauty audience that's under-served.
  • The Northeast is already working. VT (2.08), ME (1.85), MT (1.74), NH (1.66) — JRB massively over-indexes. Bobbi's audience is here. No incremental spend needed.
  • Southern states are a double gap. MS, AL, LA have low CDI AND low BDI. But LA is interesting — CDI is 0.99 (near average) with a very low BDI of 0.71. Worth testing.
  • JRB retail stores over-index nearby states. NJ (1.32 BDI), CT (1.44), CO (1.42) — all have stores. Physical retail lifts digital brand interest in surrounding geos.

Plan of Action

Geo-Targeted
Awareness Campaign

A phased approach to close the brand gap in high-category-demand states, measure lift, and scale what works.

Phase 1 / Weeks 1-4

Launch geo-targeted awareness on Meta

Target Geos

Primary: CA, TX, FL, GA

35% of beauty category demand. $2.4M+ revenue gap. Budget weight by category size.

Secondary: AZ, NV, LA

Smaller markets but large BDI gaps. Include at lower budget.

Campaign Setup
  • Create a dedicated prospecting campaign with geo-targeting overlaid on existing interest/LAL audiences
  • Separate ad sets per state (or state cluster) for clean measurement
  • Exclude existing customers in each geo to focus purely on new acquisition
  • Start with 2-3 best-performing awareness/TOF creatives from existing campaigns
Creative
  • Awareness-first, not DR. Founder story, "why clean beauty," product education
  • Video > static for awareness (optimize for ThruPlay, not purchases)
  • Test 1-2 creatives with regional/cultural hooks (e.g., "Texas heat-proof" for TX)
  • Include UGC from creators in target states if available

Phase 2 / Weeks 4-8

Measure the lift

Run a clean pre/post + test/control comparison to isolate the impact of geo-targeted awareness.

Test vs. Control Design

  • Test states: CA, TX, FL, GA, AZ, NV, LA (receive geo-targeted awareness spend)
  • Control states: OK, KY, WV, MS (similar CDI/BDI profile but no incremental spend)
  • 4-week read minimum before evaluating
  • Optional: Meta brand lift study in test geos for recall/awareness data

Phase 3 / Week 8+

Evaluate, iterate, expand

Evaluate
  • Re-run BDI/CDI analysis — did Brand Index move in target states?
  • Compare test states' revenue growth vs. control states
  • Calculate incremental ROAS: (test state lift - control state lift) / awareness spend
  • Check new customer acquisition rate change in test geos
Iterate
  • Double down on states showing strongest BDI lift
  • Kill or re-creative states with no movement
  • Test layering retargeting in states that showed awareness lift but not conversion lift
  • Adapt top-performing creative for new geos
Expand
  • If lift is positive, expand to Tier 2 states: NY, IL, VA, WA, TN, MD
  • Set a goal: move target states' Market Strength from <0.70 toward 1.0
  • Consider pairing with influencer/creator partnerships in target geos
  • Explore CTV/connected TV in CA + TX for upper-funnel scale

Budget Note

This is incremental awareness budget, not a reallocation of existing DR spend. The goal is brand-building in under-penetrated geos, which has a longer payback window. Recommend 10-15% of monthly Meta budget carved out for this geo-awareness initiative.
